# Mastering Glassmorphic Presets

Glasscn ships with three distinct design presets. Each preset handles shadows, borders, saturation, and backdrop-blur differently to adapt to your backgrounds.

## ❄️ 1. Frosted Preset
The Frosted preset is the classic satin-like glass style. It uses:
- High backdrop-blur (`12px`)
- Soft white border color for contrast
- Medium opacity overlay

## 💧 2. Liquid Preset
The Liquid preset mimics clear crystal reflection. It features:
- Low backdrop-blur (`6px`)
- Higher color saturation boost (`140%`)
- Subtly highlighted glowing borders on hover

## 🌑 3. Matte Preset
The Matte preset is a heavy, dark-tinted overlay. Best used for:
- Modal background overlays
- Dropdown menus
- Dashboard container sidebars
